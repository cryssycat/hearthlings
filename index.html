Hearthlings

Small comfort guardians born from warmth, wishes, and cozy magic.

Species Overview

Hearthlings are tiny magical companions about the size of a house cat. They are drawn to people, homes, and places that need comfort, protection, or a little extra warmth. Their appearances vary wildly depending on the traits they are born with, from soft plush bodies to glowing lantern tails and starry eyes.

Size

Small house-cat height

Role

Comfort guardians and tiny magical companions

Magic

Gentle protective magic, emotional warmth, and cozy charms

Setting

Homes, cafes, gardens, libraries, festivals, and magical towns
